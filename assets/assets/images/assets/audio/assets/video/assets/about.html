About Our Thrift Store

September 11, 2025

Our Story

The USIU Thrift Store was founded to promote sustainability and affordability on campus.

Key milestones in our journey:

  1. Founded in August 2024 by USIU students
  2. First pop-up event in September 2024
  3. Permanent store opened in January 2025
  4. Expanded to include household items in June 2025

What We Offer

Our thrift store provides:

  • Clothing for all USIU students
  • Textbooks and academic materials
  • Household items and dorm essentials
  • Electronics and accessories
  • Sustainable shopping options

Thrift Store Terminology

Sustainable Fashion
Clothing designed to be environmentally friendly and ethically produced
Pre-loved Items
Previously owned items that have been donated or resold
Circular Economy
An economic model that emphasizes reuse, recycling, and minimizing waste
Fast Fashion Alternative
Sustainable clothing options that counter the disposable fashion industry

Our Impact

Since opening, we've diverted over 500kg of textiles from landfills!
